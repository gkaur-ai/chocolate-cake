HOW TO MAKE CHOCOLATE BUTTERCREAM

  1. ⅓ cup butter, at room temperature
  2. ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, divided
  3. ⅓ cup milk
  4. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  5. 1 (16 ounce) package confectioners' sugar, sifted

Step1

Beat butter and 1/4 cup cocoa powder together in a bowl with an electric mixer on low until light and fluffy.

Step2

Gradually add milk, remaining 1/4 cup cocoa powder, and vanilla extract; beat until smooth.

Step3

Add confectioners' sugar, about 2 tablespoons at a time, until desired consistency is reached.

Step4

Use the buttercream :)
