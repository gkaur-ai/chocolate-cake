Tips
Common mistakes to Avoid

  1. Overmixing toughens texture.
  2. Measure flour by spooning, not scooping.
  3. Use room temperature ingredients.
  4. Do not skip boiling water. It blooms cocoa flavor.
  5. Test oven temperature accuracy.
  6. Opening oven door early causes sinking. Patience yields height.

    FAQs

  1. Can I make chocolate cake without eggs? Yes, use applesauce or flax eggs as substitutes. Batter adjusts with extra liquid. Results stay moist.

  2. How do I fix a sunken cake?
    3. Avoid early oven peeks. Ensure proper measurements. Fresh leaveners prevent sinking.
  3. Is Dutch-processed cocoa okay? It works but alters color and rise. Natural cocoa best for this recipe.

  4. Can I bake in advance? Yes, wrap unfrosted layers. Freeze up to three months. Frost day of serving.

  5. Why is my frosting grainy? Sift sugar well. Beat longer for smoothness. Use softened butter.
