HOW TO MAKE THE BEST CHOCOLATE SPONG/BASE

Chocolate cake is a dessert that has a chocolate flavor and chocolate frosting. The history of chocolate cake is not clear, but there are many stories about its invention. One report says that a chef in France made the first chocolate cake for Napoleon Bonaparte‘s wife, Josephine. Another story says that a woman in the United States named Sarah Lee invented chocolate cake in the 1800s.
  1. 2cups(240g) Cake flour/ all purpose flour
  2. 3/4 cups (88.5g) Unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. 3/4 teaspoon (3g) Baking powder
  4. 2 teaspoon (8g) baking soda
  5. 1 teaspoon (5.6g)
  6. 1 teaspoon (4.2g)vanilla extract
  7. 1 3/4cups (350g) Granulated sugar
  8. 3 large Eggs: room temperature
  9. 1/2 cup (109g) Vegetable oil
  10. 1cup (240g) Buttermilk or regular milk
  11. 1cup Hot coffee

Step1. Prep the pans

Preheat your oven to 350°F. Grease and line your cake pans with parchment paper for easy release.

Step2. mix the interdients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined. In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, buttermilk, oil, water (or coffee), and vanilla

Step3. Combine and Bake

With the mixer on low, slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. The batter will be thin, and that’s exactly what you want. Thin batter = ultra-moist cake. Divide the batter evenly between prepared pans. Bake for about 28–32 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

Step4. Allow to Cool

Let cakes cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then turn out onto cooling racks. Cool completely before frosting.
